Media Statements

Netlink launches promotion to support SMES and drive digital transformation

1 September 2020 – NetLink Trust (“NetLink”) is offering promotional pricing for its Non-Residential Connection Service (“Non-Residential Service”) to Service Providers targeted at reducing fibre broadband cost for small and medium enterprises (“SMEs”).

NetLink’s offer is aimed at helping Singapore SMEs accelerate their adoption of digital technologies during this challenging COVID-19 period. Participating Service Providers will receive rebates for each new Non-Residential Service connection that they applied for their SME customer. The rebates will be in the form of a waiver of the Monthly Recurring Charge (“MRC”) for the Non-Residential Service for a period of 12 months. NetLink hopes that its offer will help Service Providers create more competitive fixed broadband offers to SMEs, at a time when many businesses are badly affected by COVID-19.

Interested SMEs may approach their Service Providers and enquire about NetLink’s offer. This promotion is valid for new orders from 1 September 2020 till 28 February 2021.

Mr Tong Yew Heng (唐耀兴), Chief Executive Officer of NetLink, said, “As a Singapore entity, it’s important for us to do our part to help local businesses impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic. Many SMEs are affected by the economic downturn brought on by the pandemic. We hope that SMEs will take advantage of this promotion and leverage on the high-speed internet access offered by NetLink’s network to conduct their businesses online and accelerate the digitalisation of their businesses.”

For more information, please click here.

NetLink Trust designs, builds, owns and operates the fibre network infrastructure which is the foundation of Singapore’s Next Generation Nationwide Broadband Network.
