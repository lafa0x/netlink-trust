Fibre Broadband For Earthworks Contractors

Before Any Digging Starts

Before embarking on any excavation works in Singapore, all earthworks contractors are required under the Telecommunications Act to obtain information on the actual location of existing fibre telecommunications cables. The objective is to prevent accidental damage of existing telecommunications cables in the course of the earthworks (refer to the Telecommunications Act). Damaging the telecommunications cables will not only delay the earthworks, services will be disrupted and the errant earthworks contractor will have to pay for the repair cost.

Purchase Plant Route Plans

The actual party carrying out the earthworks must purchase Plant Route Plans (PRP) which indicate the location of NetLink Trust’s existing underground plant in the vicinity of the proposed site developments. You will need the PRP in order to apply for the Notification for Commencement of Earthworks (NCE); you are directly responsible for declarations, confirmations and information provided. You must engage a licensed Telecommunications Cables Detection Worker (TCDW) to detect and mark out the location of existing NetLink Trust’s underground plant at the site.

With effect from 7 Nov 2020, you are required to register for a new account and submit your Plant Route Plan (PRP) purchase requests via the new PRP Portal.

For all existing purchase requests for Plant Route Plan (PRP) submitted before 6 Nov 2020, please access the information via the existing PRP Portal.

For any PRP enquiries, please contact cpe@netlinktrust.com

Apply for Notification for Commencement of Earthworks

After detecting existing NetLink Trust’s underground plant at site, earthworks contractors have to submit Notification for Commencement of Earthworks (NCE).

With effect from 7 Nov 2020, you are required to register for new accounts and submit your NCE applications via the new NCE Portal.

For applications for Notification for Commencement of Earthworks (NCE) submitted before 6 Nov 2020, you can access your applications via the existing NCE Portal.

For enquiries on existing applications, please contact cpe@netlinktrust.com
